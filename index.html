How it works

Up and running in minutes

No training required. TurfWize is built for people who work outside, not behind a desk.

1

Add your clients and properties

Enter a client, add their property address, and draw the boundary on the satellite map. Takes under 2 minutes per property.

2

Schedule jobs and optimize your route

Create jobs for the day, tap Optimize Route, and TurfWize builds the most efficient order automatically.

3

Dispatch your crew

Assign crew members to each job. They see their tasks on their phone and you track progress in real time.

4

Invoice and get paid

When a job is complete, generate an invoice in one tap and send it directly to your client.
